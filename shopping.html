The Shopping List

This is the list of items you'll need to get started with the project.

Items needed only during the setup phase:

  • USB keyboard
  • HDMI cable
  • HDMI TV or computer monitor
  • SD card reader

Items to keep the weather station running:

  • Raspberry Pi
  • USB Power supply (to power the Pi)
  • Micro USB cable (to connect the Pi to the power supply)
  • SD card (about 8GB is a good size)
  • A USB Wireless Weather Station.
    I have successfully used thes models:
    • Clas Ohlson Weather Station
    • Maplin USB Wireless Weather Forecaster
