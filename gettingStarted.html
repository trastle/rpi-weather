Getting started with your Raspberry Pi

First you’ll need to download an operating system for your Raspberry Pi. For this tutorial I have chosen Raspian “Wheezy”, an easy to use Linux based operating system which is endorsed by the Raspberry Pi foundation and seems to be very popular. You can download a copy from the Raspberry Pi Downloads Page.

Once you have downloaded the image you need to copy it onto your SD card. I suggest you follow the relevant RPi Easy SD Card Setup instructions at Elinux.org.
